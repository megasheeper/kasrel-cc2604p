Result:
"Fashion & Face: K-Collabs Glow-Up A K-pop Themed Event Focusing on Fashion and Cosmetics
Project Title: Fashion & Face – K-pop Edition Student Name: [Kasrel] Class / Course: [CCP] 
1. Introduction
The “Fashion & Face” project explores the exciting intersection of fashion and cosmetics (beauty). This version transforms the event into a vibrant K-pop themed experience, using two popular groups to represent the two main elements:
•	NewJeans – The “Face” (glowy cosmetics, dewy makeup, and luxury brand collaborations).
•	CORTIS – The “Fashion” side (raw, punk-inspired street style and self-expression from their track “FaSHioN”).
Event Name: K-Collabs Glow-Up Tagline: Glow with NewJeans and add edge with CORTIS!
The event educates attendees on how K-pop idols influence global trends while offering fun, hands-on activities.
2. Objectives
•	Showcase real brand collaborations in fashion and beauty through K-pop.
•	Provide interactive workshops on makeup and styling.
•	Highlight the contrast between polished luxury (NewJeans) and rebellious punk energy (CORTIS).
•	Build creativity, confidence, and appreciation for K-pop culture among young people in Singapore.
•	Create an engaging, shareable event that mixes learning and entertainment.
3. Theme & Concept
Overall Vibe: Fresh, youthful glow mixed with bold punk attitude.
•	NewJeans brings effortless dewy skin, glossy lips, natural looks, and high-end collabs (Chanel, Gucci, Dior, Burberry, Louis Vuitton, Levi’s, Armani Beauty, YSL Beauty).
•	CORTIS brings DIY punk fashion – graphic tees, layered outfits, chains, distressed pieces, and raw attitude from their 2025 BigHit Music debut.
This mix makes the event dynamic and balanced.
4. Target Audience
Ages 15–25, especially K-pop fans in Singapore and Southeast Asia. The event is designed to be affordable and highly photogenic for social media.
5. Event Activities & Zones
Suggested Duration: 1 full day (e.g. Saturday, 11:00 AM – 6:00 PM) Possible Venue: mall pop-up in Singapore like Orchard Ion.
Main Zones:
1.	K-Collabs Runway Show Models or student volunteers showcase NewJeans luxury collab outfits and CORTIS punk street styles. Live commentary explains the brands and styling. Ends with a dance performance to NewJeans and CORTIS songs.
2.	Glow-Up Makeup Workshops (NewJeans Focus) Hands-on sessions teaching dewy skin, glossy lips, and fresh looks inspired by NewJeans members and their beauty collaborations (Hanni with Armani/Gucci Beauty, Minji with Chanel Beauty, Danielle with YSL Beauty, etc.).
3.	Punk Fashion Styling Station (CORTIS Focus) “FaSHioN” Challenge: Create edgy, self-expressive looks using affordable or thrifted clothes. Focus on attitude and creativity.
4.	Interactive Photo Booths & Exhibition Themed backdrops – elegant NewJeans campaign style vs. urban CORTIS punk vibe. Props for trying on looks.
5.	Trend Talk Session Short discussion: “How NewJeans and CORTIS Are Shaping Global Fashion & Beauty Trends”.
6.	Merch & Take-Home Zone DIY stickers, temporary tattoos, custom tote bags, and small K-beauty samples.
6. Sample Schedule
•	11:00 AM – Doors open + photo zones
•	12:00 PM – Runway Show 1
•	1:00 PM – 4:00 PM – Rotating workshops and styling stations
•	3:00 PM – Trend Talk
•	4:30 PM – Runway Show 2 + dance segment
•	5:30 PM – Closing and group photos
7. Marketing Ideas
•	Hashtag: #KCollabGlowUp
•	Promote via Instagram and TikTok with short clips using NewJeans and CORTIS music.
•	Posters featuring side-by-side NewJeans glow and CORTIS edge.
8. Expected Outcomes
Attendees will gain practical skills in makeup and styling, understand K-pop’s industry impact, and feel more confident in expressing themselves.
9. Simple Budget Outline
•	Venue and basic setup
•	Props, makeup samples, and small prizes
•	Marketing (mostly digital – low cost) The budget can be reduced with sponsorships from local beauty shops or fashion brands in Singapore.
10. Conclusion
This K-pop themed “Fashion & Face” event perfectly combines fashion and cosmetics using the fresh energy of NewJeans and the bold style of CORTIS. It delivers a fun, educational, and memorable experience suitable for young audiences in Singapore.
